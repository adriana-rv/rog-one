The workshop

The ROG workshop is organized as part of the Work Package 3 of CONNECT, a project supported by the European Union's Horizon 2020 programme for research and innovation. The workshop intends to bring together a group of 15 to 20 researchers to work together on open problems related to Restricted Orientation Geometry. The problems are to be proposed by the organizers and the participants. Attendance to the workshop is by invitation.
